Underlines

The link underlines of my dreams would let you:

  • change the line thickness
  • change the distance from the baseline of the text
  • (maybe) change the colour
  • skip descenders rather than cut through them (though there is debate on how desirable this is)
  • be consistent across browsers

As-is

You can’t keep a pet pig at your home until you get a county parish holding (CPH) number from the Rural Payments Agency (RPA). You must also tell the Animal and Plant Health Agency (APHA) that you’re keeping pigs, within 30 days of your first pet pig arriving on your land.

  • Handled very differently by different browsers
  • Underline gets too thick at larger sizes

Text decoration colour

You can’t keep a pet pig at your home until you get a county parish holding (CPH) number from the Rural Payments Agency (RPA). You must also tell the Animal and Plant Health Agency (APHA) that you’re keeping pigs, within 30 days of your first pet pig arriving on your land. 

text-decoration-color: #939497
  • Supported by 93% of browsers
  • Intention is to use a lighter colour than the text to make the line look lighter
  • Underline colour needs to have 3:1 contrast against background (I think?) so can't be much lighter than the existing blue... so maybe pointless
  • Same issues as above – handled differently by different browsers and goes too thick at larger sizes

Text decoration thickness + offset

You can’t keep a pet pig at your home until you get a county parish holding (CPH) number from the Rural Payments Agency (RPA). You must also tell the Animal and Plant Health Agency (APHA) that you’re keeping pigs, within 30 days of your first pet pig arriving on your land. 

text-decoration-thickness: 1px
text-underline-offset: 2px
  • Can control line thickness and distance from baseline
  • Not widely supported – both text-decoration-thickness and text-underline-offset reach around 17.6% of users (view in Safari or Firefox to see the effect)

Border bottom

You can’t keep a pet pig at your home until you get a county parish holding (CPH) number from the Rural Payments Agency (RPA). You must also tell the Animal and Plant Health Agency (APHA) that you’re keeping pigs, within 30 days of your first pet pig arriving on your land. 

border-bottom: 1px solid $govuk-link-colour
text-decoration: none
  • Sits too far below baseline – halfway between the lines of text
  • Can't change the underline postition without messing up the line height

Box shadow + text shadow

You can’t keep a pet pig at your home until you get a county parish holding (CPH) number from the Rural Payments Agency (RPA). You must also tell the Animal and Plant Health Agency (APHA) that you’re keeping pigs, within 30 days of your first pet pig arriving on your land. 

background-size: 1px 1em
box-shadow: inset 0 -2px #fff, inset 0 -3px $govuk-link-colour;
text-decoration: none;
text-shadow: -1px -1px #fff, -1px 1px #fff, 1px -1px #fff, 1px 1px #fff;
  • The text and box shadows need to be set as the colour of the background – so you'd need to adapt it for anything other than a white background
  • Possible performance issues with box shadow and text shadow?
  • Looks weird/buggy when you select some text

Background image + gradient + text shadow
